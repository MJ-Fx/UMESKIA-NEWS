Frequently Asked Questions

You can submit news tips by emailing newsroom@umeskia.news or calling our news tip line at +254 700 654 321. All tips are confidential, and we protect our sources.

For advertising inquiries, please contact our sales team at advertising@umeskia.news or call +254 700 789 012. Our team will provide rate cards and help you develop an effective advertising strategy.

We take accuracy seriously. To report an error, please email corrections@umeskia.news with the headline of the story, the date it was published, and details of the correction needed. Our editorial team will review all correction requests.

You can subscribe to our newsletter by visiting the newsletter subscription page or checking the "Subscribe to our newsletter" box when submitting the contact form. We offer several newsletter options covering different topics.

Current job openings are listed on our careers page. To apply, send your resume and cover letter to careers@umeskia.news. For internship inquiries, please specify your area of interest in the subject line.
