Our Mission

At Umeskia News, we believe in the power of information to transform lives and strengthen communities. Our mission is to deliver journalism that is:

  • Accurate

    Facts verified by our team of experienced journalists

  • Balanced

    Multiple perspectives on every important story

  • Transparent

    Clear about our sources and methods

  • Community-Focused

    News that matters to the people we serve

Our History

2010

Founded as a Local Blog

Umeskia News began as a small blog covering community events in Nairobi, founded by journalist Michael Umeskia.

2014

First Digital Platform

Launched our first dedicated website, expanding coverage to national news and increasing our team to 5 full-time journalists.

2018

Regional Expansion

Opened bureaus in 3 additional East African countries, establishing ourselves as a regional news leader.

2022

Current Platform Launch

Rebranded and launched our modern digital platform, reaching over 2 million monthly readers across Africa.

Our Editorial Values

We verify information with multiple sources before publication. Our fact-checking team reviews all content, and we promptly correct any errors with transparency.

Umeskia News maintains strict editorial independence from advertisers, sponsors, and political interests. Our journalism is not for sale.

We actively seek out underrepresented perspectives and ensure our coverage reflects the diversity of our communities.

We hold power to account through investigative reporting and follow-up on important issues until resolution.
